MatthewBradley

I'm a model & prop maker with a keen affinity for sculpting, with a life-long love of stop-motion animation and its associated artistry.

My work breathes life into history, Tolkienesque fantasy, and English folklore. These passions culminate in creations that blend ancient whispers with modern craftsmanship.

"My ideal life? That of a forest-dwelling Hobbit wood carver!"
