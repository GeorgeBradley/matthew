The Magic of Stop-Motion

Stop-motion animation brings inanimate objects to life through meticulous craftsmanship and frame-by-frame movement. From hand-sculpted puppets to intricately designed props, every piece is a work of art that tells a story.

Puppets and Props

Puppets are the heart of stop-motion animation. They are crafted with precision, often featuring wire armatures for poseability and detailed textures to mimic real-life materials. Props and models complement the puppets, creating immersive worlds that captivate audiences.
